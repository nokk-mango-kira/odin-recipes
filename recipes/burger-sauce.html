Burger Sauce

Description

Tangy burger sauce that will take homemade hamburgers up a notch. It also pairs great will chips and fries!

Ingredients

  • Codiments: A flavorful mixture of ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard is the base
  • Seasonings: This burger sauce recipe is seasoned with dried minced onion, dried minced garlic, and seasoned pepper.
  • Hot sauce: Hot sauce (to taste) spices things up.
  • Vinegar: A dash of vinegar adds a bit of zing and balances the other ingredients.

Directions

  1. Whisk all ingredients together
  2. Chill in the fridge for at least an hour before server

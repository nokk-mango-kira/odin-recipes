Normal Pancakes

Description

Pefect pancakes that are easy to make, This recipe will produce think, fluffy, and all=around delicious pancakes with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • White sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk and butter
  • Egg

Directions

  1. sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides
